Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text sent to any server?
No. All transformations happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device.
What text transformations are supported?
We support 11 case conversions (UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, kebab-case, CONSTANT_CASE, dot.case, path/case) plus Reverse text, Remove extra spaces, Sort lines (A–Z and Z–A), Remove duplicates, Trim whitespace, Add line numbers, and Remove empty lines.
Is there a character or usage limit?
No. Use it as much as you want with text of any length. Since processing is local, very large texts may take a moment but there is no enforced limit.
